Company Overview

Business Profile

Robinhood Markets, Inc. is a modern financial services platform that provides commission-free trading of stocks, ETFs, options, and cryptocurrencies through a mobile app. Founded with the mission to democratize finance, the company designs its own products and delivers them through a single, app-based cloud platform.

The company's vertically integrated platform has enabled the introduction of new products including cryptocurrency trading, dividend reinvestment, derivatives, fractional shares, and recurring investments. Robinhood has recently expanded into banking and wealth management services as well as prediction markets with NCAA tournament futures.

Robinhood has demonstrated impressive growth with 24.8 million funded customers and a 106% increase in assets under custody. The company has achieved four consecutive quarters of GAAP profitability, with Q4 2024 showing revenue up over 100% and net income increasing over 10x year-over-year. Their recent $45 million SEC settlement has resolved previous regulatory issues, paving the way for continued expansion.
