Company Overview

Business Profile

SoFi Technologies is a digital financial services company offering a comprehensive range of products including lending, banking, investing, and financial education services. Founded initially as a student loan refinancing company, SoFi has expanded its offerings to become an all-in-one financial platform targeting primarily younger, digitally-savvy consumers.

The company recently achieved profitability in 2024 and continues to show strong growth metrics, with a 34% year-over-year increase in members (10.1 million total) and a 32% year-over-year increase in products (14.7 million total). SoFi has been actively diversifying beyond its core lending business, finalizing a $5 billion loan platform agreement with Blue Owl Capital and continuing to expand its financial services and technology platform offerings.

Operating in the highly competitive fintech space, SoFi competes with both traditional banks and other fintech companies. Its differentiation comes through its all-digital platform and comprehensive product offerings designed for tech-savvy consumers seeking an integrated financial experience. The company's banking charter, enabling deposit-taking capabilities, represents a significant competitive advantage among fintech companies.
